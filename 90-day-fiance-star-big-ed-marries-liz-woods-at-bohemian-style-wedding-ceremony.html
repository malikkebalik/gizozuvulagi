'90 Day Fiance' star Big Ed marries Liz Woods at bohemian-style wedding ceremony

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

BENTONVILLE, ARKANSAS: '90 Day Fiance' stars Ed Brown aka 'Big Ed' and Liz Woods have finally tied the knot together. The couple swapped vows and were pronounced married on Tuesday, August 29 in Bentonville, Arkansas.

The wedding ceremony was attended by the couple's friends and family.

The couple has been navigating the choppy waters of their love for a long time now. But marriage and commitment could heal the couple's relationship, which has seen numerous break-ups, followed by teary-eyed reconciliations. 

The wedding ceremony was reported to have a bohemian theme, complemented by a strict 'no white' dress code.

The venue of the evening was at Holland Barn. The reception started at 3 pm and went on till around 10 pm.

How did '90 Days Fiance' stars Big Ed and Liz Woods meet? 

Big Ed aka Ed Brown first met Woods at Encontro, a restaurant he frequented and where Woods worked as a waitress manager.

Woods was impressed by Brown's respectful and comfortable behavior with her little daughter Ryleigh. They fell in love. But they split for the first time, both under the impression that the timing wasn't right.

However, as time passed, Brown and Woods decided to give it another go and announced in November 2021 that they were engaged.

They appeared together in the 90 Day franchise's 'The Single Life'. They have broken up with each other multiple times before finding out that they can't live without each other. 

Who is Big Ed's first wife? 

According to reports, Ed Brown has been married before and his first wife was Sandra Heckman. This was before he embarked on his '90 Day Fiance' journey. The divorce is said to have happened in January 1992.

Following their split, both have tried their best to look after their daughter Tiffany. Ed Brown's mother, Norma Brown, has also taken care of the child and provided for Tiffany's needs over the years.

Heckman is currently said to live in Las Vegas, working as a Director of Maintenance/Customer Service for the Prescott Companies. Her current husband's name is Bruce Heckman.  

Who are Big Ed's children? 

Though Brown's marriage to his ex Heckman lasted only two years, they welcomed a daughter called Tiffany. As of 2023, Tiffany is thirty-one years old and works in the personal assisting and fitness industry, along with having a real-estate business. 

'90 Day: The Last Resort' Episode 5 airs on Monday, September 11, 2023 at 9 PM EDT.
