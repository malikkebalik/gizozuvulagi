Who is Alex De Rakoff?

Alex De Rakoff is a British director as well as a writer and producer. Alex De Rakoff noted works are two comedy movies titled The Calcium Kid (2004) and Dead Man Running (2009).

Alex De Rakoff also has directed two video games Need for Speed: The Run in 2011 and Transformers Universe in 2014.

Who are the parents of Alex De Rakoff?

His birthdate is 13 Nov 1970 and he was born in London, England, the UK as Alexander Arkadi De Rakoff. The name of his mother and his father’s identity is not published.

Further, Alex De Rakoff’s ancestry is English, and has an older sister named Sophie De Rakoff. She is a costume designer who has worked in hit movies like Legally Blonde, In Her Shoes, Sweet Home Alabama, etc.

As per his academics, the details are not mentioned.

Alex De Rakoff- Professional Life, Career

Alex De Rakoff in 1999 directed a short video named GTA 2: The Movie. Before this, he has worked in music videos named “If I Was Your Man” (1995), “Stayin’ Alive” (1995), “Boys Don’t Cry” (1996), “Walk Like a Champion” (1996), “Afrika Jam” (1996), etc.

But his most noted work is related to two films. In 2004 and 2009, he directed plus wrote two comedy films named The Calcium Kid and Dead Man Running respectively. These projects of his were appreciated by the audience.

Alex De Rakoff as a producer has produced 19 episodes of Snatch (TV Series) and a TV documentary Queen: Live at the Rainbow in 1992. His other-directed music videos are,
