How Do You Get Cauliflower Ear?

Cauliflower ear is permanent, says Healthline. 

It is caused due to a direct blow to the outer ear. When the blood or other fluids fill the space between the ear, it disrupts the normal blood flow.

These can also occur when skin is pulled away from the cartilage. 

Most often, the cauliflower ear is related to sports injuries while any trauma to the ear can also result from it.

For example, an ear infection can lead to blocking in the blood flow that may affect the cartilage resulting in the cauliflower ear.

In only a few cases we can reverse it and is possible through a procedure called otoplasty.
