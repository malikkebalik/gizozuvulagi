Who are the parents of Giancarlo Esposito?

Giancarlo Esposito was born on 26th April 1958 in Copenhagen, Denmark. He is the son of his mother Elizabeth Foster and father Giovanni Esposito.

His mother worked as a nightclub singer and his father was an Italian stagehand as well as a carpenter.

He attended Elizabeth Seton College in New York. From there he earned a two-year degree in radio and television communications.
