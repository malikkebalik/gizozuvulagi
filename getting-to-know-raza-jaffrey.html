Getting To Know Raza Jaffrey

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Raza Jaffrey is a well-famed actor who is renowned as Dr. Neal Hudson in a 2015 television series, Code Black. At present, he is filming a TV mini-series, The Serpent Queen, and a TV series, Pantheon.

Jaffrey was born on 28th May 1975. He is six feet (183 cm) tall and has a pair of brown eyes that perfectly suits his shiny black hair.

Jaffrey's Homeland

The English actor was born in Wallasey, Merseyside in England. However, his father refers to Agra, India while his mother is a native of Liverpool.

This way, Jaffrey is related to both India and England.

Who Are Jaffrey's Parents?

The Refuge actor was born to his father Hyder Jaffrey, and his mother Joan Bradshaw. Talking about the professional life of Hyder, he used to be a sea captain of his time.

However, Joan has not gone public regarding her occupation.

Where Did Jaffrey Graduate From?

Jaffrey is one of the notable alumni of Dulwich College. Later in his life, he attended Manchester University where he studied English and Drama.

Soon, he got admitted to Bristol Ols Vic Theatre and graduated from there with a degree in acting.

Career Outset of Jaffrey

America's Next Top Model contestant made his debut in acting from a television show, EastEnders, in 1999. Later in 2004, he starred for the first time on the big screen. It was the movie Dirty War where he portrayed Rashid Dhar.

Jaffrey has always proved himself perfect for every character he is provided with. In today's date, he is among the actors who have spotless careers without controversies.

Net Worth of Raza Jaffrey

Jaffrey has a total net worth of $1.5 million as of 2022. He is a well-paid actor and has earned a handsome sum of money from his acting career.

Additionally, the net worth of his present wife is $2 million. 

Jaffrey's Ex-wife

Jaffrey got married to Miranda Raison in September 2007. Raison was his co-actor in the movie, Spooks. 

However, things did not go right and the pair separated their ways in November 2009.

Jaffrey's Present Wife

After Lost in Space actor divorced from his first wife, he came across another English actress, Lara Pulver. They began dating each other in 2012. Jaffrey and Pulver took vows on 27th December 2014. The married couple is filming a new movie, Pantheon.

Lara was also an actress in Spooks together with Jaffrey and Raison. Besides, she was previously married to Josh Dallas in 2007 but divorced in 2011.

Does Jaffrey Have a Son?

It is reported that the GALECA award nominee shares a son with his current wife Pulver. However, very few details are known about his son. 

Sweet Girl actor's son, Ozias Jaffrey, was welcomed in February 2017, three years after their marriage.

Raza Jaffrey on Social Media

Jaffrey is available on Instagram and Twitter. However, he has not got an official Facebook page. Raza is followed by more than 14.2k fans on his Instagram profile.
