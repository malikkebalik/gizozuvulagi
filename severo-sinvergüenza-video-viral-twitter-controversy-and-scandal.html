Severo Sinvergenza Video Viral Twitter | Controversy And Scandal

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Waffles and sweets in the form of male genitalia are served at Severo Sinvergüenza in Cali. Additionally, the staff members here have a unique approach to serving the consumers. Waffles’ form inspired people to start creating videos that have become popular. Viewers’ responses to the videos are inconsistent. The owner of the business has received a lot of attention on social media, and many people have noticed the ladies who are the subject of the commercials.

Twitter Severo Sinvergüenza Video

A Cali, Colombia, business called Severo Sinvergüenza that sells pastries and waffles in phallic shapes has lately gained notoriety on social media and in the media. The restaurant’s unusual method of serving clients, which entails mimicking romantic scenes with sweets, has caused it to become viral. The video that started the viral craze showed a team member removing what seemed to be condensed milk with his tongue after spreading it over a woman’s breast.

Since then, a ton more films of a like sort have been posted on Twitter, enhancing the hype around the venue. The emphasis on the store has also drawn attention to one of its staff members, a 27-year-old Venezuelan named Ray Cabrera. Ray has established himself as a prominent character and has been in several ads for the bakery.

His films are being shared with friends by the audience, which has been popular for a while. While the video has drawn criticism, many individuals have appreciated it despite the controversy. Both men and women have been seen standing in a huge line to get dessert from the restaurant. People are prepared to stand in line to experience the shop’s distinctive items, which demonstrates its rising popularity.

The Severo Sinvergüenza Scandal and Controversy

Despite the controversy and criticism surrounding the institution, it doesn’t seem that it has affected their patronage, especially among women. At least 300 ladies stood in line at the store, and some of them said they enjoyed it without feeling humiliated, according to a journalist from Caracol Radio. The store posted a statement in Spanish on its Facebook page in reaction to the trending footage on social media.

They claimed to have educated their staff to treat customers with respect and acquire their permission. Severo also highlighted that it is forbidden to engage in any behavior that blurs the line between enjoyment and sensuality.

They disclaim any liability as a brand for such conduct within the customer’s premises. The statement ended by promising that the necessary steps will be taken to stop such situations from happening again.

They said that they would be more cautious moving forward with their goods and would not let any other films become as popular as the one being used currently. The fact that so many people like the sweets the business serves has increased interest in testing them out, especially among women. Long queues of clients are now waiting to take advantage of the establishment’s distinctive offers as a result of this enthusiasm.
