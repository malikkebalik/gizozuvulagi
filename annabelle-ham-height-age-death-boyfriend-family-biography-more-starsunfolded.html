Some Lesser Known Facts About Annabelle Ham

  • Annabelle Ham was an American YouTuber, TikToker, and Social Media Influencer, who was famous for her lifestyle-focused content on YouTube. She delighted her devoted viewers by showcasing her stylish outfits, sharing beauty secrets, and providing expert makeup tips that kept them coming back for more. On 15 July 2023, Annabelle Ham tragically passed away following an epileptic seizure. Prior to the news of her untimely death, there were reports of her going missing in Fairhope, Alabama, as she had travelled there to partake in a bachelorette party organized for her friend.
  • She grew up in Atlanta, United States of America.

    Annabelle Ham in childhood with her mother and sisters

  • Annabelle Ham kickstarted her YouTube journey on 13 May 2014, establishing a self-titled channel to share her creativity with the world. Her content was a delightful blend of challenges, pranks, DIY projects, beauty tips, glimpses into her life, and hauls, all expertly designed to captivate her audience and leave them craving for more. The channel amassed an impressive following of around 80.5k subscribers.

  • Additionally, she owned another channel called “AnnabelleHamVlogs,” where she documented her daily life adventures, gathering a dedicated audience of 7.6K subscribers by 2023.
  • Among her popular uploads was a song lyrics prank video featuring Taylor Swift‘s “Look What You Made Me Do,” which added to her fame and popularity.
  • In addition to her YouTube success, Annabelle Ham boasted a considerable fan following on Instagram and TikTok.
  • Leveraging her influence, she collaborated with several brands, including Clean & Clear, Fab Lectics, and Air Heads Candy, promoting their products to her engaged audience on Instagram.

  • During her leisure time, Annabelle Ham found immense joy in both travelling to new destinations and indulging in the pleasures of reading books.
  • Fondly calling her fans, her “HamFam,” she embraced a close and affectionate connection with her dedicated audience.
  • Table Rock Management served as the reliable representative and management agency for Annabelle Ham, handling her professional endeavours and career aspirations.
  • As a passionate dog enthusiast, Annabelle Ham displayed her love for furry companions by sharing numerous pictures with dogs on her Instagram account. Among them was her beloved pet dog named Redd, who held a special place in her heart.

    Annabelle Ham with a puppy

  • She was seen partaking of alcoholic beverages on different occasions.

    Annabelle Ham enjoying her drink at a bar

  • On the 15 July 2023, Annabelle Ham tragically passed away after experiencing an epileptic event, which is a disorder of the brain characterized by recurring seizures. The sombre news was disclosed by her sorority chapter at Kennesaw State University. Prior to her body being discovered in Fairhope, Alabama, where she had gone to attend a bachelorette party for a friend, her family had filed a missing report at a local police station in Alabama. According to the police, she was last seen walking on a pier at the end of Molokai Lane in Fairhope. A few days after her demise, her family shared a heartfelt Instagram post on her page, confirming the sad news to her fans and followers. Accompanied by a family picture, they expressed their grief and sorrow and wrote,

    This is Annabelle’s family. We’re writing this with heavy, heavy hearts. Annabelle experienced an epileptic event and has gone on to the gates of heaven. She struggled with this for a long time and wanted to raise awareness for it, which we will do in honour of her.”
