Naomi Watts: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

Naomi Watts was born as Naomi Ellen Watts in Shoreham, Kent, England, the UK on September 28, 1968.

Her father, Peter Watts died because of a heroin overdose when she was seven years old. He was a sound engineer for Pink Floyd and Myfanwy Edwards, a dealer in antiques, also engaged in set and costume designing.

Her mother’s name is Myfanwy Edwards Roberts. She has a brother, Ben Watts. After her father’s death, her mother raised her and her brother in Australia.

Later, her mother married again in 1978 and they lived in Suffolk.

Her brother Ben is an English photographer for Elle, Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Vanity Fair, and Condé Nast Traveller.

She has British nationality and is of Caucasian ancestry.
