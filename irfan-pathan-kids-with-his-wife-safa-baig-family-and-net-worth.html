Cricket enthusiasts are interested in learning more about talented former cricketer Irfan Pathan kids and his wife, Safa Baig. 

Ifran Pathan, a former Indian cricketer, was born on October 27, 1984, in Baroda, Gujarat, India, to father Mehmood Khan Pathan and mother Samimbanu Pathan.

Pathan did his schooling in Baroda, where he was considered a bright kid. He was very interested in sports and started playing cricket while in school.

He made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2003 and quickly established himself as one of the top players and as a bowling all-rounder in the country. 

His contributions were crucial in India’s triumphs in the 2007 ICC Twenty20 World Cup and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Irfan is a legendary cricketer and a loving husband and father. As a family man, he is equally proud of his two sons, Imran Pathan and Suleiman Pathan.

Moreover, the 38-year-old is a hands-on dad who takes an active interest in his kids’ upbringing and is always there to support and encourage them in whatever they do.
