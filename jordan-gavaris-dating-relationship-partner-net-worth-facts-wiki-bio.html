Jordan Gavaris Dating, Relationship, Partner, Net Worth, Facts, Wiki-Bio

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Jordan Gavaris is a Canadian actor who made his debut in the movie 45 R.P.M. in 2008. Gavaris is popularly known for his role on the TV series, Orphan Black.

Jordan has recently come out to the public as gay and also revealed some personal information about his relationship. Know more about Jordan in the following article.

Who is Jordan Gavaris?

Jordan James Gavaris, more popularly known as Jordan Gavaris was born on September 25, 1989, in Caledon, Ontario, Canada. He is the youngest child of a Greek immigrant father and a Canadian mother who is of northern European descent.

Jordan attended Mayfield Secondary School and graduated from there while having an interest in movie direction early in his life.

Gavaris made his debut in the movie G2G: Got to Go! in which he gave voice to the character Barry. However, his actual feature film debut came in the same year 2008, when he portrayed the character of Parry Tender in the movie 45 R.P.M.

Jordan has 14 credits to his name as an actor to date, with three of the projects in the stage of post-production. He is most famous for his portrayal of Felix Dawkins on the TV series Orphan Black. The series ran from 2013 to 2017 and received many positive critical responses.

Gavaris has worked in various other productions like Take Two (TV series) (2018), Unnatural History (TV series) (2010), The Sea of Trees (2015) (Movie), and many more.

Jordan Gavaris’s Personal Life: Is He In A Relationship?

Jordan Gavaris, much to the disappointment of many lady fans out there, is gay and is in a relationship with Devon Graye. They have been in a romantic relationship since 2013, but Jordan revealed this only very recently.

Jordan was named one of Playback Magazine‘s Top Ten to Watch for 2010. Past names have included Nina Dobrev and Xavier Dolan.

Breaking the news and breaking hearts

While it might have been very hard for Jordan’s female fans to know that he is gay, the actor himself actually hid the secret from the mainstream media for a very long time.

Jordan revealed that he was gay to his family when he was 19, but admits that he was never asked about his sexual orientation before an interview on Vulture.

It’s a scary thing to have to own who you are publicly. I think that’s largely why I waited. I always kind of knew if somebody asked I would start talking.

Jordan finally came out to the public back in February of 2017, when he was finally asked about his sexuality. He said in the interview that it shouldn’t matter if he is gay or not in the show business. He also admitted that he thinks he will get fewer parts in movies just because he is gay.

Jordan Gavaris has an estimated net worth of about $1 million. He earns this money through his acting jobs and modeling stints.
