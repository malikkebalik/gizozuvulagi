American Dancer, Lili Avagyan Shares Her Marital Relationship With Reggie Bush.

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 8, 2024

Lilit Avagyan is a prominent Armenian Dancer who rose to prominence after being married to a famous American retired footballer, Reggie Bush. Besides it, she is known for her dancing skills and also won the 2010 Rising Star Open dance competition as well as other reputed dancing competitions as well.

The beautiful dancer, Lilit was born on 30 November 1987 in Armenia, under the birth sign, Sagittarius. Likewise, she belongs to the Armenians ethnicity and holds an American-Armenian nationality.

Married To Reggie Bush

As of now, Lili is living happily with her husband, Reggie Bush who is a famous retired American footballer who used to play as Running back for different well-known football teams such as New Orleans Saints, Detroit Lions, and Buffalo Bills. The lovey-dovey couple exchanged their wedding vows on 27 July 2014 in front of their close friends and relatives. Similarly, Reggie's ex-girlfriend, Kim Kardashian stepbrother, Brody Jenner, attended their wedding along with his girlfriend, Kaitlynn Carter, and shared a picture of them on his Instagram.

Their wedding ceremony was held at Grand Del Mar Resort, which is one of the most expensive and luxury hotels in San Diego, California. The NFL player, Reggie joked about the marriage ceremony saying, "The wedding is costing me a lot of money." 

The two first met in 2011 and started dating since then. As of now, the couple is sharing a healthy and lovable marital life, and she often shares pictures of them on her Instagram.
