Kez Sunday Udoka Father

Kez Sunday Udoka’s father, Ime Udoka, a Nigerian-American professional basketball coach and former player. He was born on August 9, 1977, in Portland, Oregon, USA. Udoka played college basketball at the University of San Francisco before starting his professional career in 2000.

After retiring as a player in 2012, Udoka began his coaching career as an assistant coach with the San Antonio Spurs. He has also served as an assistant coach for the Philadelphia 76ers and the Brooklyn Nets. In June 2021, Udoka was hired as the head coach of the Boston Celtics, becoming the first head coach of African descent in the franchise’s history.
