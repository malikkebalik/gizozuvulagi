What were the names of Shawn Grate's victims? The Talks Today

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

In a forthcoming episode of its true-crime series Living With A Serial Killer on Oxygen, the show will revisit and look into the terrifying case of serial killer Shawn Grate. The second season’s fifth episode will premiere on Oxygen on Saturday, August 6, 2022 at 9 p.m. ET/8 p.m. CT.

This post has explicit details regarding violence, so please read at your own risk.

According to the official summary for the episode of Living With A Serial Killer’s second season:

A rescue attempt to free a kidnapped woman becomes gruesome when two dead women are discovered inside the house; the killer confesses during questioning; and his half sister talks about the unstable upbringing that creates a serial killer.
A notorious serial killer from Marion, Ohio named Shawn Grate killed five people in cold blood. Between 2006 and 2016, he killed Stacey Stanley, Elizabeth Griffith, Candice Cunningham, Rebekah Leicy, and Dana Nicole Lowrey.

At the Chillicothe Correctional Institution, the horrible serial killer is currently serving a life sentence for his heinous murders. The execution of Grate is anticipated for 2025.

People have been interested in finding out more ever since Oxygen unveiled information about the amazing serial killer for its next episode of Living With A Serial Killer Season 2. The channel also released a preview clip, which only piqued viewers’ interest in the noteworthy specifics of the terrible victims.

Let’s get right to it and learn more about Shawn Grate’s five victims before Oxygen airs Episode 5 of Living With A Serial Killer Season 2.

Prior to the Living With A Serial Killer show on Oxygen, all information regarding Shawn Grate’s five victims

Who were the victims of Shawn Grate, and what happened to them?

Between 2006 and 2016, Shawn Grate viciously murdered 5 women.

Stacey Stanley, 43, was one of Grate’s five victims. Grate killed Stacey, also known as Stacy Hicks, by strangling her.

Her body was found at the Ashland home where Shawn was detained after her family reported her missing just one week prior to his arrest.

A another sad victim of Grate’s was Elizabeth Griffith. Griffith was 29 years old when Grate violently ended her life by strangling her; authorities also discovered Griffith’s remains at the Ashland residence.

Prior to the serial killer being apprehended, Griffith was missing for about a month.

At the age of 29, Shawn took the life of Candice Cunningham, another victim of the murderer. The murderer led law enforcement to Cunningham’s lifeless body after being apprehended. On November 1, 2016, the Richland County Sheriff’s office identified her body after it had been discovered there.

Another terrible name on Shawn’s heartbreaking list of victims is Rebekah Leicy. Only 31 years old at the time of her horrible murder by strangulation. One year prior to Shawn’s arrest, in March 2015, her body was discovered.

Although it was initially assumed that she had overdosed on drugs, Grate eventually admitted to killing Leicy in a similar manner after being found guilty.

The most recent victim of Shawn Grate’s has been identified as 23-year-old Dana Nicole Lowrey. Despite Ohio police finding her body in 2006, it was misidentified for almost 13 years. After being imprisoned, Grate admitted to strangling her.
