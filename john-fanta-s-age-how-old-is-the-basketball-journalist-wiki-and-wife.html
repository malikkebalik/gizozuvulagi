John Fantas Age: How Old Is the Basketball Journalist? Wiki And Wife

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Given the fans’ interest in learning more about John Fanta, we’ve compiled a John Fanta Wikipedia page, including his age. Fanta is a rising sportscaster making a name for himself. This page covers his age, educational background, career, and marriage.

John Fanta, a dedicated basketball journalist, has been in media since childhood. Aside from his basketball expertise, he’s known for his humor and fashion sense. He works as a play-by-play announcer for Fox Sports and hosts Big East Shootaround, a weekly digital program on the Big East Digital Network, Twitter, and YouTube. Fanta’s passion for sports journalism began in high school and continued through his college years.

He attended Saint Ignatius High School, where he covered over 200 athletic events on television. Fanta’s knowledge extends to the prominent figures in these events. Additionally, he served as the voice of the Pirate Sports Network, covering contemporary Seton Hall sports teams. Fanta stands out for covering both men’s and women’s sports, setting him apart from other reporters.

Exploring John Fanta’s Wikipedia And Age

John Fanta was reportedly born in 1995, making him 28 years old as of 2023. His exact birth date remains unknown. Fanta, originally from Hoboken, New Jersey, possesses extensive radio expertise. After graduating from Saint Ignatius, he earned a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Journalism from Seton Hall University in 2017. While maintaining an impressive 3.75 GPA, he also managed the Pirate Sports Network, streaming over 50 Seton Hall home athletic events per year.

Additionally, he worked as a sportscaster and news anchor for Seton Hall’s student radio station, 89.5 WSOU. Fanta is not only a journalist but also a dedicated religious participant, serving as a lector and cantor at weekly masses for the university community.

John Fanta’s Career

In 2015, Fanta made his national television debut on FOX Sports, with subsequent appearances on FOX Sports 1 and FOX Sports 2. He covered college sports including basketball, soccer, baseball, and softball. Since November 2018, he has been a FOX College Hoops Play-by-Play Broadcaster and previously worked for the BIG EAST Conference for nine years in various capacities.

He also served as a Sports Production Intern for FOX in 2016, creating highlight packages for MLB Whiparound and international soccer programming. 

Regarding his personal life, Fanta is happily married to his wife, Victoria Rizzo Fanta, a qualified dental hygienist. They tied the knot on September 1, 2023, and according to their wedding website, their love story is nothing short of a fairytale. Due to COVID-19, Fanta couldn’t travel for work, which led to their serendipitous meeting during the Christmas season.

To initiate a conversation, John asked Victoria if she was single, and after a brief chat, they planned a date. Fanta proposed on May 13, 2022, and they got married a year later. They are yet to start a family and enjoy activities such as horseback riding together.
