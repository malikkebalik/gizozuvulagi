Biography

Jake Hoffman was born in Los Angeles, CA on March 20, 1981. Known best for his role as the version of Ben Newman in the 2006 Adam Sandler feature Click, Hoffman also appeared with his famous father, Dustin Hoffman , in the HBO series Luck. In addition, he acted in the 2013 Martin Scorsese film The Wolf of Wall Street. He co-starred with Devon Aoki in a 2009 indie film titled Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Undead. He graduated in the early 2000s from New York University’s Film School.
