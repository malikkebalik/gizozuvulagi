Sara Bareilles is an American singer, songwriter, actor, musician, author, and producer. In 2004, she released her debut studio album Careful Confessions which included the songs – Love On the Rocks, Gravity, Fairytale, Come Round Soon, Responsible, and others. She then went on to release many other albums including Little Voice, Kaleidoscope Heart, What’s Inside: Songs from Waitress, The Blessed Unrest, More Love: Songs from Little Voice Season One, and Amidst the Chaos. Moreover, Sara Bareilles has also been cast in a variety of shows and movies like Jesus Christ Superstar Live in Concert!, Girls5eva, Little Voice, Lipstick Jungle, 30 Rock, No Ordinary Family, Sesame Street, A Trace of the Sun: Volunteering in Japan, and Girl Play. Her performance as Mary Magdalene in the NBC musical television special Jesus Christ Superstar Live in Concert! earned her nominations for the Primetime Emmy Award for “Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Limited Series or Movie” and the Grammy Award for “Best Musical Theater Album”.
