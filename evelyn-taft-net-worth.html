Evelyn Taft Wiki Biography

Evelyn Taft was born on 24 August 1984, in the San Francisco Bay Area of California, USA, of Russian – actually her first language – and Jewish descent. Evelyn is a meteorologist and journalist, best known for being a part of CBS2/KCAL 9 which is broadcast from the San Francisco Bay Area. She is a Golden Mike Award winner for Best Weather Segment in Southern California, but all of her efforts have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Evelyn Taft? As of late-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $500,000, mostly earned through a successful career in journalism. She forecasts the weather as a part of the KCAL seven-hour morning show, and as she continues her career, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Growing up, Evelyn lived in various countries, including England and France, as well as spending time in South and Central America, Israel, and around America, Asia, mostly touring. She attended Menlo School in California, and after matriculating went to the Annenberg School of Journalism at the University of Southern California. During her time there, she forecast the weather as part of Annenberg’s live newscast, so after graduating with a degree in Broadcast Journalism and Political Science, she moved on to gain another degree, in meteorology from Mississippi State University.

After completing her studies, Taft worked as a weather and news anchor for KCOY-TV. She also did field reporting, including covered several Santa Barbara County wildfires. She stayed with KCOY-TV for two years, and worked on a series entitled “Military Minute”, for which she was also rewarded with the National Army Guard Appreciation Award. Eventually, Taft worked for a time in KRON 4, before moving to CBS2/KCAL9. Her popularity grew steadily and so did her net worth, as she became a part of the network’s seven-hour morning show. She forecast the weather and eventually would win a Golden Mike Award for Best Weather Segment in Southern California. She continues to work there as a journalist, director, editor and weather anchor.

Evelyn is also a member of the National Weather Association, and has been given the Association’s Seal of Approval. For her work in reporting various wildfires, she was given an Army National Guild Award. As she continues her work, her net worth also grows with numerous opportunities coming her way. A few of her recent projects include being a part of the morning and evening newscasts alongside Stephanie Simmons, Sharon Tay and Rick Garcia.

For her personal life, it is known that Taft has been married to Ross Resnick – the founder of the online directory entitled “Roaming Hunger” – since 2011, having already been in a relationship for several years. She’s fluent in several languages including Russian, French and Hebrew. She’s also very active on social media, having a Facebook account with over 20,000 likes, and over 24,000 followers on Twitter and 25,000 on Instagram. All of her social media accounts are updated regularly.
