Ryan Gosling And Eva Mendes Three-Year-Old Daughter; Amada Lee Gosling's Interesting Facts

Being a celebrity kid is a hard thing when paparazzi always surround you. The life of Amada Lee Gosling is also the same. Amada, the three-year-old daughter of Ryan Gosling and Eva Mendes, is the second child of the couple.

If you know Ryan, then you might see that he is a big fan of keeping secrets. The couple never made an official announcement about the birth of Amada, which suggests they want to keep everything under wraps. So, if you want to know more about her, then read the article as we explore more facts about the celebrity kid.

Amada's Top Secret Birth

According to TMZ, Ryan and Eva pulled off the most significant cover-up ever in Hollywood baby history. Eva hid the baby bump very well that no one knew about her pregnancy. In every photograph, the paparazzi took, Eva, is seen hiding her belly.

With such a cover-up who knew the actress was pregnant with a child. On April 29, 2016, Amada Lee was born to her parents at 8:03 AM. As per the reports, Mendes delivered the baby at St. John's Health Center in Santa Monica. 

After the birth, the pair never made an official announcement as they wanted to keep it a secret. But as the good news got leaked, the blessings and congratulatory messages from the fans and their loved ones keep coming. 

She is the Second Child

Amada arrived in the family as the second child in 2016. In the family, she has an older sister by the name of Esmeralda Amada Gosling. The arrival of Amada has bought much happiness into the family.

Did you notice? Esmeralda's middle name is similar to the first name of Amada. Amada is a Spanish word which means loved in English. We don't know if the couple thought about this when they kept the name, but we do know that Amada is also the name of Eva's grandmother. 

The fact that there is not much information on either of the child suggests the length, Ryan and Eva went to protect their family. In an interview, Mendes said that the kids will expose to the paparazzi, but they wanted to do all they can to protect their kids from that, and Pregnancy was the start.

This means we might not get daily news about Ryan's children in the coming future as well.
