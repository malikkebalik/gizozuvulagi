Michelle Foley is an American social media star who is best known for being Ava Marie Foley’s mother and part-founder of the YouTube channel ForEverAndForAva. She also has a secondary YouTube channel titled “All In The Foley Family” to which she uploads lifestyle and family vlogs along with other engaging content. Michelle also has a huge fan base with more than 300,000 followers on Instagram.
