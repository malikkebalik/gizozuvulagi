If you have watched the American superhero film Venom, then you must've heard about Tom Hardy, who appears as Eddie Brock/Venom in the movie Venom. He is also known for playing in the Peaky Blinders.

Tom was born on 15th September 1997, London, England, where Finn Cole, an actor, was also born. Tom's parents are Chips Hardy and Elizabeth Anne Hardy. He is of White ethnicity and holds British Nationality. 

Hardy's first wife, Sarah Ward

The Dark Knight Rises actor has been in several relationships to date. In 1999, Hardy tied the knot with his first wife, Sarah Ward. 

After a few years of their nuptials, the couple began facing issues due to Hardy's drug addiction, which ended up in their separation. Sarah filed for the divorce, which was finalized in 2004. 

Tom was Allegedly in an Affair with Rachel Speed

When Tom was in a marital relationship with Sarah, he also had an affair with Rachael Speed. The pair first met on the set of the movie The Virgin Queen in 2005. 

The former pair also shares a son named Louis Thomas Hardy (born on 8th April 2008). A year later, like his first affair, Tom and Rachel started facing problems, and they decided to split in 2009.

The Revenant Actor now lives with his current spouse Charlotte Riley

After a few months of a break-up, Tom began dating an American actress Charlotte Riley. Hardy and Riley first met during the shoot of Wuthering Heights and soon fell in love. 

After dating for almost five years, Tom exchanged his wedding vows with Charlotte Riley in 2014. The following year in October, the pair welcomed their first child, whose names and details are unknown.

Similarly, their second son was born in December 2018, whose name is assumed to be Forest as per some sources.

Crazy about Bikes and Pets

Not to mention, he also owns a classic triumph bike and a white Ducati Supersport S. Moreover, he also owned two rescue dogs named Woodstock and Max. 

Tom also appeared at PETA with Woodstock. Sadly, the dog died on 5th June 2017 due to polymyositis.
