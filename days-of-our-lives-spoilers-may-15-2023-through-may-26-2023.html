Days of Our Lives Spoilers May 15, 2023 Through May 26, 2023

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 25, 2024

We have the latest Days of Our Lives spoilers from Monday, May 15, to Friday, May 26. Life takes an unfortunate twist for Paulina and Abe. Elsewhere, Megan makes herself at home, Nicole prepares to have a paternity test, Anna tells a fib, and Gabi hopes that her dream of marrying Stefan is finally about to come true!

Days of Our Lives spoilers week of May 15:

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Monday, May 15:

In today’s recap, Kate’s rescued, and Dimitri reveals another hostage.

Roman hasn’t had much to celebrate of late, but he’s finally about to get some very good news.

It’s a good news/bad news scenario for Alex. Sure, the poor guy just got fired. But on the plus side, Stephanie is there to comfort him in this stressful time!

Shawn continues to beat himself up over having shot Bo. (Could there be more to this incident than meets the eye?)

Stork alert: Brandon Barash (Stefan) reveals he’s going to be a dad again.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Tuesday, May 16:

In today’s recap, Dimitri reveals his hostage, Megan reveals a son, and Xander fears Susan’s haunting him.

When it comes to DiMeras, it seems as if there’s a new one coming out of the woodwork — or clawing their way out of a premature grave — every year or two. Well, get ready, because here comes another one!

Clearly, Megan is not a big fan of being behind bars. When she demands an update from Kristen on the plan to regain their freedom, will she hear what she’s hoping?

Even as Xander and Chloe are growing closer thanks to a vulnerable moment they share, Brady is opening up to John and Marlena about his feelings for the erstwhile Ghoul Girl.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Wednesday, May 17:

In today’s recap, Colin sets a trap for Abe and Paulina, while someone stumbles upon Nicole’s secret.

Talia’s been bonding quite a bit with Chanel and Paulina, and the last thing you ever want to do when running a con is get to know the mark. Is that why she tells Colin that she wants out of their scheme?

Upon finding out that Sloan has a brother, Eric can’t help but want to know more. (Dude, do we need to remind you about what happened to the curious kitty?) Meanwhile, Rafe and Jada are definitely becoming increasingly suspicious of Colin!

Nicole is glad to have a confidant in Anna, to whom she opens up about her pregnancy.

Twenty-three years after its last episode aired, see what Beverly Hills, 90210’s OGs look like now in stunning then-and-now photos.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Thursday, May 18:

In today’s recap, Rafe and Jada discover who Colin really is, just as he puts Abe and Paulina in grave danger.

Nicole probably knew that her secret would come to light sooner or later, but we’re willing to bet the last person she wanted to discover it was Sloan. So… um… guess who figure it out today?

Things are heating up between Chanel and Talia… and not just in the kitchen!

Primetime soap legend Heather Locklear shares a momentous turning point — plus, then-and-now photos of Melrose Place’s stars.

Days of Our Lives spoilers for Friday, May 19:

In today’s recap, Colin holds Paulina and Chanel at gunpoint on a rooftop, while Gabi and Stefan’s engagement has EJ popping his own question.

It looks as if Colin is about to take a page out of the playbooks used by many a Salem villain before him. How? By taking a hostage! At the same time, Sloan has to face a few tough questions to answer regarding her connection to Colin!

How will Chanel react when Talia finally decides to spill the tea regarding her recent actions?
