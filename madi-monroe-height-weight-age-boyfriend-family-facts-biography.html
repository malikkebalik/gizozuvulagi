Madi Monroe is an American actress, singer, social media star, and dancer who came to prominence with her appearance as Autumn in the short film Here After in 2018. However, she is also known for her being popular on her eponymous TikTok account and also for the pictures that she uploads to her Instagram account with different popular social media stars. Aside from that, she is also notably known to be the daughter of 5-time MLB All-Stars third baseman Matt Williams. With time, she has also amassed a huge fan base with more than 5 million followers on Instagram and more than 20 million followers on TikTok.
