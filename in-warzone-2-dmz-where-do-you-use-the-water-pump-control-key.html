In Warzone 2 DMZ, where do you use the Water Pump Control key?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 16, 2024

Players in Call of Duty: Warzone DMZ have the opportunity to not only discover hidden loot caches that can only be unlocked using their unique keys, but also to explore the points of interest on various maps. The second season of DMZ gave players a tonne of new content to explore with the inclusion of Ashika Island. While some players might be focused on completing brand-new Crown Faction missions, others may be searching for every key the new location has to offer.

Similar to Al Mazrah, Ashika Island has a large number of locked doors and places that must be accessed with a particular key. To finish missions or simply loot whatever is locked behind the lock, players need the keys. If players have the Water Pump Control key in their possession but are unsure of its location, we can help. We described where the Water Pump Control key can be used in Warzone 2 DMZ in this guide.

In Warzone 2 DMZ, where do you use the Water Pump Control key?

On Ashika Island, the Water Pump Control key can be used in an unspecified location. It is situated at the northernmost point of the island, in the F3 quadrant of the map. Players need to find a specific structure just off the coast in the F3 quadrant; it is the one with the green “1” icon in the screenshot below.Without a High Value Target mission, we encountered this person while exploring the DMZ map. The key fell next to their body, securing it for our own objectives.

The best course of action if you’re trying to get this key is to accept a High Value Target mission while you’re on Ashika Island and cross your fingers. While it takes some luck to focus on these keys, they are still useful targets for reducing the size of your search area.

We think a few players have also been fairly lucky in locating these keys and have picked them up from haphazardly placed bags or chests all over the island. Once you have the key, you can proceed by bringing it to Ashika Island’s Water Pump Control. It is located in the island’s northern region. Your target door will be blue with a white mark covering it. With the key, you’ll be able to access this space.

Inside, there are two entrances that can both be accessed by using the Water Pump Control key. The building has two doors, one on the west side and the other on the east. They are on the first floor, close to the street. There is a ladder on this building as well, but players cannot enter from the top. Use the Water Pump Control key to unlock whichever door you decide to enter, then enter to find the treasure inside. An Intel page, a valuable piece of loot that is necessary for some Faction missions in DMZ, is one such item.
