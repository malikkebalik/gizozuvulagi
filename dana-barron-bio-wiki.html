Dana Barron: Career, awards

Dana started her career by doing TV commercials at a young age. When she was 11 years old, she acted with Christine Baranski in Hiding and Seek on Broadway.

Then she starred in the Chevy Chase comedy National Lampoon’s Vacation, playing the role of Audrey Griswold in 1983.

In 1989, she received a Daytime Emmy Award for her appearance in the CBS after-school special No Means No

From the year 1984-1985, she appeared on the soap opera One Life to Live as Michelle Boudin.

Then she played a guest role as Nikki Witt on the Fox series Beverly Hills, 90210, during which she was able to make her character’s clothes and modify her lines.

Dana Barron had even starred with JoBeth Williams and Chris Burke in the NBC movie Jonathan: The Boy Nobody Wanted in 1992.

And has made guest appearances on TV shows including The Equalizer, In the Heat of the Night, Murder, She Wrote, and Babylon 5 as a telepath named Lauren Ashley in the Season 5 episode “The Corps is Mother, the Corps is Father”.
