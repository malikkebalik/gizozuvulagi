Gavin Creel Net Worth, Earnings, TV Series, Facts, Wiki-Bio

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 7, 2024

If you are a music fan, then Gavin Creel is quite a familiar name in the American entertainment industry. The Tony Award winner got massive limelight after his performance in Hair and Hello, Dolly!

The multi-talented superstar is active in the industry since 1998 and since then gathered thousands of fans worldwide. Not just that, he also earns a whopping net worth out of his glamorous journey. So, today, we provide you all the details regarding Creel’s professional life.

Gavin Creel is a Millionaire

The American multi-talented star owns a whopping net worth of $1 million as of 2019. Through his glamorous acting and singing profession, he manages to live a lavishing lifestyle.

Already two decades into the entertainment industry, Creel is still raring to go as far as possible which belongs to the best interest of his fans as well.

Gavin Creel’s Journey Started from Theatres

The charismatic Gavin Creel pursued his glamor career from the broadways in 2001. His first ever stage performance was Spring Awakening as Melchior in Roundabout Theatre company.

Creel since then, never looked back and featured several plays like Hair, Mary Poppins, and Waitress. He featured multiple theatrical works from 2012-2015 in The Book of Mormon. He portrayed Elder Price at different destinations for the show.

Creel in Hello, Dolly!

The 43-year-old Creel portrayed Cornelius Hackl in the famous 1967 musical Hello, Dolly! Shubert Theatre in 2017-18. 

Following the play, he received widespread positive reception from his exceptional performances. Likewise, his latest on-stage credit came earlier in 2019 as Dr. Pomatter in Waitress.

Creel, however, took a leave for a particular time from Hello, Dolly! in 2018 for medical reasons. 

Gavin Creel in Television and Films

The 43-year-old did not limit himself to broadway but also promoted himself to television in 2003. He featured in The Wonderful World of Disney movies, Eloise at the Plaza and Eloise at Christmastime as Bill. Moreover, his theatrical show, She Loves Me, was on television as well.

You could also see Creel’s production work in a short film from 2016,  The Ceiling Fan.

Creel’s Success as a Singer

The 43-year-old Creel released several albums of his songs like Goodtimenation and Get Out. Furthermore, he published some singles like Noise and Whitney Houston in 2011 and 2012. 

Creel’s singing career wasn’t limited then as he also recorded different gigs with his cast in Flashdance and Hair. 

Gavin Creel is a Tony Award Winner

Gavin Creel received the prestigious Tony Awards in best-featured actor in a musical for Hello, Dolly! in 2018. Surprisingly, he also won the Tony Award nomination earlier in 2002 and 2009.
