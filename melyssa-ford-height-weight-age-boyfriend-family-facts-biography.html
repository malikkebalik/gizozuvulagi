    Personal Trainer

    Melyssa is very committed to her fitness regime, which has helped her achieve an enviable figure. Although she regularly works out in the gym, she has credited hiking for getting her in the best shape of her life. She prefers countless scenic canyons around in California for her hiking jaunts.

    If she cannot head out for hiking, she tries to mimic those conditions on the treadmill by setting it to the highest incline and walking at a speed of somewhere between 3.2 to 3.7 mph with a 3 or 5-pound dumbbells in her hands. Sometimes, she extends her workout after completing the hike and employs a couple of resistance bands and other props.

    While working out in the gym, she particularly focuses on bodyweight exercises. For her upper body muscles, she relies on basic army pushups. To sculpt her lower body and butt, she performs a series of squats and lunges. However, she doesn’t use weight for these exercises and instead focuses on keeping the repetitions high. She usually goes for the squats and lunges combo done over 3 sets with each set comprising of 25-30 reps.

    Melyssa Ford Facts

  • Before she found success as a model and actress, she used to work as a bartender.

  • She initially had to get stitches on her head. Later, she was rushed to the hospital to get treatment for several gashes on her legs caused by the broken glass.
  • She is a tomboy and loves to race motorcycles and cars. She even owns a Yamaha R6.

  • She launched a podcast on YouTube titled I’m Here for the Food in November 2019.
