So, some couple moves in together to spend some time with their loved ones. Going to the same lane, now Ariel Winter has moved in with her boyfriend Levi Meaden.

Ariel Winter has moved in with her boyfriend Levi Meaden and also confirmed the news

Ariel Winter stepped up in her relationship with Levi Meaden. She opened up about how she moved in with her boyfriend during her appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live on Wednesday, May 10.

Ariel, a modern family actress,19, and the Aftermath actor, 29, first confirmed their relationship last November and also made their official red carpet debut the very next month.

Winter revealed that she is living with  Meaden in her 1.5 million pad in L.A.’s Sherman Oaks neighborhood. Furthermore, she said:

 “I do have my own house. Last year I bought my first house, which was really exciting for me. My boyfriend and I live together, and he cooks. I can’t cook at all. He takes care of all that handy stuff. He’s great, he does all that. I can bake a pie occasionally. Pumpkin and apple. I baked those two the other day. They were edible.”

She notifies that she is totally unknown about cooking, cleaning, and home repairs. She uttered:

 “I’m like the worst wifely person. Like I said, I bake those pies. He does everything else. It’s great.”

The actress also opened up about her plans to visit the University of California, Los Angeles, and continue a political science degree. In addition, she said:

“I want to be a lawyer, I wouldn’t give up acting. I love being an actress, I think it’s great, but I definitely want to have another skill. … I could be an actress and never get another role in my life, and as long as I have a backup plan, then I’ll be set.”

Ariel Winter celebrating her 4-month Anniversary in China Vacation

Ariel Winter certainly isn’t shy about her love for her boyfriend. The Modern Family actress took to Instagram to celebrate her 4-month anniversary with Levi Madean in China. The couple had a fun time there and they were joined by their squad and had an adventure-filled vacation for two weeks. They traveled to see the Great Wall of China and also had a night out at the Shanghai Club.
