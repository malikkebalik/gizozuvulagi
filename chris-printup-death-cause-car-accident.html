Chris Printup, commonly known as Spanto, founded Born x Raised and was a well-known individual. He recently passed away, leaving a creative and charitable legacy.

The co-founder of Born x Raised, Chris Printup, commonly known as Spanto, perished tragically in a vehicle accident.

The streetwear community, his partners, and fans are all extremely grieved by this awful tragedy.

Chris Printup received widespread praise for his important contributions to the fashion industry.

His contributions to the business were solidified by his innovative ideas and partnerships with well-known companies like New Era, Converse, and several Los Angeles sports teams, including the Lakers, Dodgers, Kings, Rams, and LAFC.

His art displayed a distinctive fusion of style, ethnic influences, and a strong bond with Los Angeles, his birthplace.

He did, however, show tremendous fortitude and tenacity. He was said to have beat cancer in 2018, impressing many with his bravery and tenacity to follow his hobbies.
