Savannah Soutas Age, Married, Husband, kids, wiki-bio.

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 1, 2024

There is not just one way to be famous. The statement is perfectly displayed by the life of Savannah Soutas who gained fame from unlikely sources. The fashion blogger was first noticed in Musical.ly (now rebranded as Tiktok). This allowed her to capitalize on the growing fan base and move to Instagram. With over 13 million followers on her Musical.ly account and over 3.8 million followers on Instagram, YouTube was the next thing for Savannah. She found success in the streaming platform and is now one of the leading creators on YouTube. 

Early Life:

Savannah Soutas was born on the 2nd of March 1993 in  Santa Clara, California. Details on Savannah’s early life have not yet been disclosed to the media. Born to Deborah Soutas, Savannah has an elder sister Chantelle Paige. Both her mother and elder sister have contributed immensely to Savannah’s success. Savannah herself is thankful for their support and is quick to laud her family every chance she gets. The social media star was into dancing from quite a young age. Her mother often jokes that Savannah was dancing by the age of 2. 

Social Media Popularity:

Savannah began gathering fans from the time she appeared in Musical.ly. This quickly translated to Instagram and YouTube. She operates a YouTube channel alongside her husband by the name of Cole&Sav. The channel alone contributes from USD 397 thousand to USD 6.4 million yearly. Alongside her social media presence, Savannah also worked as a model and a professional photographer. She used Instagram to distribute her snapshots to her growing fan base. She found popularity in the medium and decided to pursue a career in Photography. As of the current time, She has an estimated 13 million followers on her Musical.ly account and over 3.8 million followers on Instagram.

Family and Married Life:

It is no secret that Savannah values her family above everything else. She is currently married to her fellow Musical.ly star, Cole LaBrant. The pair met back in 2016 and have been together ever since. Cole who comes from Alabama is one of the members of the Vine channel, Dem White Boyz. Back when Vines was all the hype, Dem White Boyz enjoyed immense popularity on social media. The pair got engaged on the 20th of February 2017 and got married on the 9th of July 2017. Savannah shared the happy news on her Instagram alongside pictures of the pair’s wedding. 

A proud mother

Prior to her marriage to Cole, Savannah had a relationship with Tom Smith. Savannah who was 18 at the time got pregnant and gave birth to a daughter, Everleigh. This was back in 2012 and even though the relationship was doomed both Tom and Savannah tried to make things work. Tom is actively involved in Everleigh’s life and likes spending time with her. 

Following her marriage to Cole, Savannah gave birth to a baby girl, Poise Rayne LaBrant. Poise was born on the 28th of December 2018 and was revealed to the world through Savannah’s Instagram profile. The couple welcomed more family blessings with the birth of their second child a baby boy Zealand LaBrant on July 29, 2020. The family is currently living together in California.
