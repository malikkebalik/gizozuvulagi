Blueface's Girlfriend Jaidyn Alexis Gets Booed While Performing

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Blueface‘s current girlfriend Jaidyn Alexis faced a crowd of listeners who apparently weren’t too happy to see her during a recent show.

In a video that went viral on Sunday (July 17), a chorus of boos can be heard from the crowd as the mother of the “Thotiana” rapper’s first two children performed her new single “Stewie.”
