Who Is Nicki Minaj?

Nicki Minaj has been born Onika Tanya Maraj in St. James, Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago on December 8, 1982, and raised in Queens, New York City. She grew up in a violent environment with a drug-addicted father who eventually reformed after entering rehab and beginning to attend church. Minaj studied singing and acting at LaGuardia High School.

Young Money’s CEO first noticed her and later recruited her for The Carter Edition of Young Money’s own “The Come Up” DVD series. Her rapping abilities drew the attention of Lil Wayne, who eventually collaborated with her on several mixtapes.

Nicki Minaj Before & After Plastic Surgery

Nicki has gone a long way since her humble Bronx roots. We’re here to observe how she’s developed in more ways than one, from Red Lobster waitress to gorgeous Hip Hop legend. Here’s a timeline that demonstrates some significant changes since her big break in 2009, complete with photos to back it up!

1. Nose Job (2009)

Nicki has always had a sharp nose, based on earlier images, but why does it appear thinner now? She attributes it to the contouring’s power! She addressed the concerns head-on in a 2013 interview with Extra’s Renee Bargh.

“They’ll notice the contour and assume you had nose surgery, but look at ‘RuPaul’s Drag Race’ to see how you can make your nose seem any shape you want.” People assume I’m doing insane things to my skin when they see my makeup, but it’s just cosmetics.”

2. Teeth  Surgery (2010)

She was more than pleased to show off her dazzling whites on the red carpet when her Pink Friday album was released! In an interview with The Guardian, she was asked if her teeth were natural, and she responded by asking, “Are your teeth natural?” Most likely, she’s had veneers placed in her teeth to give her that megawatt grin!
